About our client:





The Illawarra Shoalhaven Local Health District (ISLHD) is an organization that provides a wide variety of health services to over 400 000 residents along the South coast of NSW, ranging north to Helensburgh all the way down south towards Ulladulla. It was formed in 1st January 2011 after the dissolution of South Eastern Sydney Illawarra Area Health Service. It is the region largest employers with 7300 employees that runs 8 hospitals such as Bulli, Wollongong, Port Kembla and Shellharbour hospital and services like aged care, cancer, drugs and alcohol addiction etc.

ISLHD aims to serve the community in terms of healthcare and wellbeing that includes both physical and mental health, as they aim “To provide best practice health care and programs that promote the health and wellbeing of people in the Illawarra Shoalhaven”(Illawarra Shoalhaven Local Health District About us,). Part of that includes providing the necessarily programs and services to deal with mental health in the Illawarra. Mental health has become a controversial topic and has become a topic of crucial research. Though its widespread, there are not many services out there to support people with mental illness or monitor it in the Illawarra. Because of this, ISLHD has required Codify Solutions to make a program that will provide assistance to school teachers by providing them interventions in dealing with mental illnesses. This is vital as research shows that 1 in 7 children suffer from mental health problems. This is unnerving as children are still in their development leaning phase and should have good mental health. The Software would list all the mental health problems along with information like behaviour, analysis and interventions. This software would be useful in supporting mental health in the region along as a start off base for more projects in the future to improve mental health locally.
